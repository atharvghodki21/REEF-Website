Ecolens

Ecolense : the first event of Prakriti-21 was organised on 23rd july 2021.

Natural Q-riosity

Our planet's alarm is going off, it's time to wake up and take action!! Natural Q-riosity : a quiz competition was organised by team prakriti as a part of REEF Summer Events to spread awareness and clear misconceptions among the young generation about the Environment and Climate crisis.

I Dare to Care

Antone france, an 18th century french poet and author of various classic novels once said something along the line that "until one has loved an animal - a part of one's soul remains unawakend" And since REEF is all about protecting the environment and the animals we share our planet with are part of the environment as much as we are - this event "I DARE TO CARE" was proposed as a part of REEF - SUMMER EVENTS 21 to encourage people to share and encourage people to be more kind towards our voiceless friends

Awoken Chronicles

The journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step! Awoken Chronicles - the final event of Prakriti-21 was a storytelling adventure game organised by REEF volunteers. The adventure revolved around a prophecy about the apocalypse with the final message that - No Devin intervention will save humanity unless we take measures to save our environment and ourselves.
