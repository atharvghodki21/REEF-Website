    About Us

    REEF (Shri Ramdeobaba college Engineers for Environment Forum) was set up as an environment club of the college by its Mentor Faculty Dr. Deepshikha Mehra and a group of like-minded students. The club, founded on 25 January 2012, is hosted by the Department of Humanities of RCOEM. The current Faculty Mentor of the club is
    Dr. Swaroop Laxmi Mudliar ; Assistant Professor, Department of Chemistry, RCOEM. The motive behind creating particular organisation was to create awareness and organise various activities for the conservation of enviornment
    • To create awareness and take up various activities for the conservation of various aspects of the environment.
    • To establish active association with various organizations working for environmental conservation.
    • To take up various activities for underprivileged children and in the process initiate and ingrain the value of environmental conservation.
    • To encourage the development and execution of ideas on role of technology for protection of environment.
    Dr. S.L. Mudliar
    Mentor Faculty

    Activities

    NATURE TRAIL

    To walk in nature is to witness a thousand miracles. Jungle safaries are arranged for reef members to various tiger reserves, biodiversity parks, etc.

    COLLECTION DRIVES

    Clean and green make a perfect pair. To keep our mother Earth clean and have a fresh environment, various collection drives are organised. Such as manja collection, waste collection, nirmalya collection and many more...

    SOCIAL AWARENESS

    Alone we can do little, but together we can do much. Hence, we encourage the people in society to give their bit for the nature. We run social awareness campaigns on social media handles, awareness rallies, etc.

    MAGAZINE

    Every year REEF come up with a magzine created by the members. All the orators, photographers, painters of the club take part and make the magzine complete for all our ecoactivists.

    Vermicomposting

    REEF in coordination of Chemistry department of RCOEM took a new initiative to do start a research program regarding Vermi composting; and build-up a research environment in our college.

    PRAKRITI

    Prakriti is the annual event of REEF. Under this we organize various events, activities and competitions for the nature enthusiasts and lovers. This is a platform where participants can showcase their talents and skills.

    UPCOMING FOCUSES

    Cleaning Water

    Water is important source of life. Our water bodies are getting filthy day by day. Hence, REEF will increase its frequency to conduct cleanliness drives near water bodies.

    Social Outreach

    Reaching towards maximum number of people. Interacting with maximum number of people and educating under privileged and meeting with their needs.

    Forest Cleaning

    Unfortunately beautiful forests are getting filthy due to plastics and other wastes. Cleanliness drives in some part of forest will be our upcoming focus.

    Paper Recycling

    Small steps taken by millions can make a big difference on ground. The leftover pages of used notebooks can be utilised to create a new book.

    Tree Plantation

    If we can't do great things, do small things in a great way. We all can plants and nurture trees atleast on our birthdays to contribute for environment.

    Waste Management

    Arrange various activities and sessions in schools, colleges and universities highlighting the importance of waste management.

    Member activities

    Work constantly for the preservation of environment. Increase the reach of the club among masses and bring laurels to the club.

    LET'S WORK TOGETHER

    Alone we can do so little, together we can do so much.
